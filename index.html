Grandma's Chicken Soup

Perfect for cold days. Feeds 6-8.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (about 4 lbs)
  • 4 carrots, peeled and diced
  • 4 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups egg noodles
  • Fresh dill, salt, pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Place whole chicken in a large pot. Cover with broth and 4 cups water.
  2. Bring to boil, then reduce to simmer. Cook 1.5 hours until chicken falls off bone.
  3. Remove chicken, shred meat, discard bones and skin.
  4. Add vegetables to broth. Simmer 15 minutes.
  5. Add noodles and shredded chicken. Cook 8 more minutes.
  6. Season with dill, salt, and pepper. Serve hot.

Dad's Famous Chili

Won the neighborhood cook-off three years running. Feeds 8-10.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ground beef (80/20)
  • 1 lb hot Italian sausage
  • 2 cans (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (15 oz) kidney beans, drained
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained
  • 2 bell peppers, diced
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tbsp cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 bottle dark beer (optional)

Instructions

  1. Brown beef and sausage in a large dutch oven. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add onion, peppers, garlic. Cook 5 minutes until softened.
  3. Add spices, stir 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add tomatoes, beans, and beer. Stir well.
  5. Bring to boil, then reduce to low simmer.
  6. Cook at least 2 hours, stirring occasionally. Longer is better.
  7. Serve with shredded cheese, sour cream, and cornbread.

Note: All recipes tested at sea level. Adjust cooking times for altitude.
